How does this work?
  • LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) is a deep-learning model trained on the last 2 years of closing prices. It learns temporal patterns and sequence dependencies.
  • Prophet is Meta's time-series model that decomposes trends, weekly seasonality, and yearly seasonality for robust forecasting.
  • RMSE (Root Mean Squared Error) measures prediction accuracy on held-out test data — lower is better.
  • Results are for educational purposes only and should not be used for financial decisions.
